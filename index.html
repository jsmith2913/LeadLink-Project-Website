        <h2>I. Executive Summary</h2>

        <p>

          Oak Valley Community Bank’s (OVCB) commercial lending lead generation and CRM preparation processes are

          largely manual and fragmented. The current Quest CRM implementation cannot download intent and contact data

          together and only tracks lead counts. An Excel spreadsheet is used as a middle layer between Quest CRM,

          RelPro, and Reonomy, introducing errors, consuming analyst time, and causing missed opportunities.

        </p>

        <p>

          OVCB’s lead conversion rate is estimated at approximately 8%, and each lead takes 30–35 minutes to enter and

          configure properly. Analysts spend significant time manually combining data from Reonomy and RelPro, cleaning

          and reformatting it for Quest CRM, and drafting outreach emails.

        </p>

        <p>

          LeadLink is a centralized, web‑based pre‑CRM application that eliminates the Excel middleman and integrates

          Reonomy and RelPro directly. Data is extracted into a Firebase database, transformed into Quest CRM‑compatible

          formats, and enriched with custom outreach emails generated via the ChatGPT API using a predefined template.

          Key features include:

        </p>

        <ul>

          <li>Data integration across Reonomy and RelPro</li>

          <li>Internal database as a secure, single source of truth</li>

          <li>Automation of data preparation and CRM export</li>

          <li>Email outreach automation per lead</li>

          <li>Expanded CRM summaries and metrics</li>

        </ul>

        <p>

          The proposed solution is expected to significantly increase efficiency and accuracy. With an estimated

          implementation cost of $5,000, LeadLink would save 8 hours per week for 2 analysts, yielding annual labor

          savings of about $37,400 and an estimated $15,000 in additional revenue from improved lead conversion.

          Security is enhanced by keeping data internal, and expanded metrics provide deeper insight into lead

          performance. The new system is projected to increase the lead conversion rate to around 12%.

        </p>

        <p>

          By overhauling OVCB’s restricted and wasteful current system, LeadLink will enhance lead generation, automate

          data inputs, and generate summaries and emails for each lead—transforming OVCB’s process and substantially

          improving operational efficiency.

        </p>

      </section>

          Lead generation begins in two applications: Reonomy and RelPro. Both are B2B prospecting intelligence tools

          with different focuses:

        </p>

        <ul>

          <li><strong>Reonomy:</strong> Real estate lead generation</li>

          <li><strong>RelPro:</strong> Lead intent data</li>

        </ul>

        <p>

          Leads are generated based on OVCB’s criteria and stored in each system. An employee then transfers lead

          information from both applications into a master Excel spreadsheet, combining data for the same leads and

          businesses and adding notes. The Excel sheet is manually formatted to fit Quest CRM and then uploaded.

        </p>

        <p>

          From Quest CRM, senior staff select leads and perform additional research. Qualified leads are passed to

          lenders, who contact prospects and begin the lending process. This workflow takes about 10 minutes per lead,

          with the bank handling 25–30 new leads per week. Analysts spend 5–6 hours per week solely on formatting and

          data handling—time that could be redirected to qualifying and prioritizing leads. On average, it takes around

          two days for a lender to contact a lead.

        </p>

      </section>

      <section class="section" id="proposed-process">

        <h2>V. Proposed Process Analysis</h2>

        <p>

          The proposed solution is a centralized, web‑based application that automates OVCB’s lead generation and CRM

          preparation. The application integrates directly with Reonomy and RelPro APIs, allowing users to search,

          retrieve, and manage leads in a single interface. It merges contact and intent data from RelPro with real

          estate data from Reonomy and stores leads in a Firebase database as a live, editable source of truth.

        </p>

        <p>

          Users can browse, annotate, and selectively export leads in Quest CRM’s required format. For each lead, a

          custom outreach email is generated using the ChatGPT API and a predetermined template, eliminating 5–10

          minutes of manual email drafting per lead. The backend handles CRM formatting, email generation, and data

          transfer, while the frontend provides an intuitive interface for search, review, export, and note entry. This

          structure reduces manual labor by an estimated 75–85%, minimizes human error, and ensures CRM‑readiness from

          the outset.

        </p>


        <h3>From manual to automated workflow</h3>

        <p>

          Currently, users log into Reonomy and RelPro separately, download CSVs, merge data in Excel, manually clean

          and format files for Quest CRM, and draft emails. This process consumes 12–15 analyst hours per week, with

          1.5–2 minutes per lead spent on reformatting and 5 minutes per lead on email drafting. OVCB processes around

          250 leads per month, resulting in roughly 21 hours per month lost to data formatting and email preparation

          alone.

        </p>

        <p>

          Delays in entry cause up to 30% of leads to go cold or be lost entirely—about 75 missed opportunities per

          month. Assuming an average commercial loan size of $250,000 and a net interest margin of 3.35%, this equates

          to approximately $627,000 per year in missed loan revenue.

        </p>

          <li>Access a single web app to search across both Reonomy and RelPro APIs</li>

          <li>Curate and review results in real time</li>

          <li>Automatically merge and clean data</li>

          <li>Add internal notes in‑app and save them to the database</li>

          <li>Export CRM‑ready lead packages instantly</li>

          <li>Download or send custom outreach emails with one click</li>

        </ul>

        <h2>VI. Solution Assessment</h2>

        <p>

          LeadLink supports OVCB primarily by reducing the time and effort required for the initial stages of lead

          generation. As one stakeholder noted, “we get paid high salaries, but find ourselves spending a large chunk of

          our time on lead generation which any low‑level employee could do as well.” By replacing the master Excel

          sheet with an interactive dashboard, time is saved at multiple points in the process.

        </p>

        <p>

          Automated data transfer addresses the largest pain point: manually entering data from one application to

          another without meaningful modification. With an estimated implementation cost of $5,000, LeadLink is

          projected to save 8 hours per week for 2 analysts, resulting in annual labor savings of $37,400 and an

          additional $15,000 in revenue from increased lead conversion.

        </p>

        <p>

          Email generation further accelerates the process once leads reach lenders. A simple, consistent starting email

          shortens the time to first contact by several days, enabling high‑level employees to manage more leads. All

          employees involved in lead generation benefit by spending more time validating and prioritizing leads rather

          than simply moving them through the pipeline.

        </p>


        <h3>Critical success factors</h3>

        <ul>

          <li>Reliable data integration from Reonomy and RelPro</li>

          <li>Automated CRM‑ready output in Quest’s required format</li>

          <li>Effective email automation with minimal need for manual editing</li>

          <li>User‑friendly UI for fast lead search and review</li>

          <li>Clear time and cost savings to justify ROI</li>

        </ul>

        <h2>VII. Conclusion</h2>

        <p>

          The LeadLink system offers a highly feasible and impactful solution for OVCB’s commercial lending operations.

          By replacing a manual, Excel‑based lead generation workflow with a centralized, web‑based platform, the bank

          can realize substantial gains in efficiency, accuracy, and lead conversion.

        </p>

        <p>

          Financially, LeadLink is compelling: a $5,000 implementation cost, annual labor savings of $37,400, and

          increased loan revenue of $15,000 support an ROI of over 400% within five years and breakeven in under 270

          days. Technologically, the system leverages stable, well‑documented APIs (Reonomy, RelPro, ChatGPT, Firebase)

          and is designed for scalability and long‑term reliability.

        </p>

        <p>

          Operationally, LeadLink enhances existing workflows without disrupting roles or requiring new hires. It

          empowers analysts, managers, and lenders by automating low‑value tasks and freeing time for strategic

          decision‑making. From a scheduling perspective, an 8‑week implementation timeline is realistic with clear

          scope and responsive collaboration.

        </p>

          Ultimately, LeadLink addresses core pain points in OVCB’s lead management process by reducing lead

          abandonment, cutting manual work by up to 85%, and enabling faster, more effective client outreach. It is a

          profitability.
